Hello There!

This website was created by two students who share a passion for celebrating the beauty and spirit of the University of Georgia's Athens campus. Our goal is to provide a helpful guide for graduates, families, and friends by highlighting some of the most picturesque and iconic photo spots around campus. From the historic Arch to the serene Founders Memorial Garden, these locations hold special meaning for UGA students and alumni alike. We designed this site not only as a practical resource but also as a way to honor the memories and experiences that make our time at UGA so special. Whether you're capturing your achievements or simply exploring the campus, we hope this guide inspires you to create lasting memories of your own.

- IDAN
